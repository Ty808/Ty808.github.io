About Me

Living my best life one day at a time.

  • Military Family

    Life as a Navy Brat

    I come from a military family. My father served in the US Navy for 23 years and instilled his core values and life skills in me. I didn't grow up the way most people do. I grew up moving to a new state, a new city every 2 - 3 years even living aboard.

  • Kidney Transplant

    Lifes First Major Curve Ball

    Probably the most shocking point in my life so far. I discovered I needed a Kidney Transplant 3 weeks before Graduating from Rad Ford High School. I was in the process of joining the navy when I was told to "Go to the hospital, you have stage 4 kidney failure". I didn't let this stop me from continuing to pursue my goals. While I could no longer join the service. I went on to Kapiloani Commnuity College for 2 semesters studing Information Technology before having the transplant surgery 3 days after my 20th birthday.

  • American Intercontinental University

    Bachelors in Marketing & MBA in Finance

    I moved to AIU becuase of there excellent online program and because there was too much uncertaitysurrounding my trasnplant. It was here at AIU where I discovered I did not enjoy information technology and had a niche for marketing. I graduated in Febuary of 2020 and took classes in Consumer Behavior, International Marketing, Marketing Campaign and Strategic Planning and many others. Not long after completing my Bachelors I started my MBA in Finance, I'm set to graduate in December of 2021 and currently taking two classes in Finance Analysis and Managerial Marketing.

  • World Travels

    Europe and Asia

    While Living in Guam I traveled to Japan and South Korea with Guam High School for events with the NJROTC program. This gave me the unique chance to see these countries and experience the culture. I 2017 I spent a month traveling around Europe, traveling to Italy, Austria, France, Germany, and Switzerland.
